About Our Own Camp

We aim to solve the issue of dependence for our consumers. It is said that the generation of primary schoolers and secondary schoolers are in the “strawberry” generation overprotected by parents.

The term “strawberry” is used to explain the people of this generation because strawberries are bruised easily. It means to say that the people of this generation, born between 1980 and 2010, are soft and easily bruised.

Our business aims to resolve this problem by letting the consumers go through an intensive camp which teaches how to be dependent on others and have discipline, as well as teaches values like the importance of family and dependence.

The camp will be divided into two groups, aged 10-12, and 13-15
