Step One

Creating an account is for us to contact you conveniently when a camp is registered or when there is a camp that might interest you!

Step Two

By posting the details of your desired camps, everyone will be able to see it. Please be precise about any expectations or if there are any extra information that might be useful in organising your camps!

Step Three

Sharing with your friends is one of the best ways to get more people on board to participate in the camp. With more people participating in the camps, the cheaper the camps will be priced at!

Step Four

Wait for us to organise it! When we have enough interested users who are interested in the particular camps, we will get in touch with different people to help make the camp a reality! Corporations are welcome to organise bonding camps for your workers! Schools and organisations are welcome to organise camps for their classes too!
